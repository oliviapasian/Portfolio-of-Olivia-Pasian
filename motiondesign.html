Origins of the Printing Press, 2022


This illustration was completed for a class assignment. The brief was to create a 1 minute video where the information is related to time, such as a historical timeline or a study of plants or animals over time. For this assignment, I chose to focus on the origins of the printing press and their relevance today. I created a timeline of significant printing inventions and added text animations to highlight key pieces of information. I also added transition animations and illustrated animations to make my video more engaging and to illustrate some of the inventions for visualization. My concept was to create my timeline in a notebook setting to create a more storytelling feel and make it more fun. My goal was to create an informative, engaging video about the origins of the printing press, and I feel that my final video fulfilled the goals of the assignment and my own goals. Created using Adobe After Effects, Procreate, and Illustrator.
